Maze Editor

In the maze editor tab, you can edit or create your own maps. There is a button on the lower right corner to make a new maze. Above it is the list of available mazes. There is a toolbar for various maze templetes on the left. In mouse mode, you are able to click on walls with left click and remove walls with the right click. When on a templete you are able to change the orientation with either a middle click or the R key. The mouse wheel make the templates grow and shrink.
